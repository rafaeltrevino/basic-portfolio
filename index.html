About Me

Rafael is currently enrolled in the Coding Boot Camp offered at George Washington University learning to become a full-stack web developer. He is also a doctoral student of Interpretation at Gallaudet University, where he is researching the effects of immediate feedback on students of translation and interpreting.

For the past 15 years, he has worked as an interpreter and translator, and his working languages are American Sign Language (ASL), Spanish and English. Rafael is originally from Florida, but now resides in Washington, DC.
